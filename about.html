ABOUT KYamedia

KYamedia Bookstore is the famous bookstore in the world. Starts from Indonesia to all booklovers all around the world. Founded by Kenny's Brother in 2015. KYamedia first store located in Jakarta. Books, Ebooks, Encyclopedia, Novels, Literatures, and many other books in there.
